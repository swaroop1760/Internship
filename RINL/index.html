Who we are

Rashtriya Ispat Nigam Limited (RINL), the corporate entity of visakhaptnam Steel Plant is a Navaratna PSE under the Ministry of Steel. Visakhaptnam Steel Plant fondly called as Vizag Steel. It is the first shore based Integrated Steel Plant in the country and is known for its quality products delighting tthe customers. It is a market leader in long products and caters to the needs of diverse Industrial sectors. It is the first Steel Plant to be certified ISO 9001:2008 (presently 2023), ISO 14001:2004 (presently 2023), OHSAS 18001:2007 and ISO/IEC 27001:2013 Standards. It is also the first PSE to be certified ISO 50001:2011 - Energy Management Systems and has acquired CMMI Level 3 Certification for s/w development.
